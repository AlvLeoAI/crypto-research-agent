# Trusted News Sources

## Source Reliability Tiers

### Tier 1: Official Sources ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Always reliable for project-specific news**

| Source Type | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| Official blogs | blog.ethereum.org, bitcoin.org |
| Official Twitter/X | Verified project accounts |
| GitHub releases | Official repositories |
| Foundation announcements | Ethereum Foundation, Bitcoin Core |
| Governance forums | forum.arbitrum.io, gov.uniswap.org |

**Usage**: Primary source for protocol updates and official announcements.

---

### Tier 2: Major Mainstream Media ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Reliable for verified news, may lag on breaking stories**

| Outlet | Strengths | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Bloomberg | Institutional, regulatory | Paywalled |
| Reuters | Breaking news, global | Fast but brief |
| Wall Street Journal | Financial analysis | Paywalled |
| Financial Times | Regulatory, institutional | Paywalled |
| CNBC | Market coverage | Sometimes sensationalized |
| Forbes | Feature stories | Variable quality |

**Usage**: Good for regulatory news, institutional adoption, major events.

---

### Tier 3: Crypto-Native Media ⭐⭐⭐
**Good coverage but may have biases or conflicts**

| Outlet | Strengths | Potential Biases |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| CoinDesk | Comprehensive coverage | Industry ties |
| The Block | Data-driven, research | Subscription focused |
| Decrypt | Accessible writing | Can be promotional |
| Cointelegraph | Wide coverage | Clickbait tendencies |
| The Defiant | DeFi focused | DeFi-optimistic |
| Blockworks | Institutional focus | Pro-crypto lean |

**Usage**: Good for crypto-specific developments, verify major claims elsewhere.

---

### Tier 4: Analyst & Researcher Sources ⭐⭐⭐
**Valuable for analysis, less for breaking news**

| Source Type | Examples | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Research firms | Messari, Delphi Digital, Glassnode | Often paywalled |
| On-chain analysts | @lookonchain, @EmberCN | Verify data independently |
| Protocol researchers | Independent auditors, security firms | Technical focus |

**Usage**: Technical analysis, on-chain data, deeper dives.

---

### Tier 5: Social & Community Sources ⭐⭐
**Useful for sentiment, unreliable for facts**

| Platform | Useful For | Caution |
|----------|------------|---------|
| Crypto Twitter/X | Breaking news, sentiment | Rumors, manipulation |
| Reddit (r/cryptocurrency, project subs) | Community sentiment | Echo chambers |
| Discord servers | Project updates | Unofficial claims |
| Telegram groups | News aggregation | Scams prevalent |

**Usage**: Sentiment gauge only. Never cite as fact source.

---

### Tier 6: Avoid ❌
**Unreliable or compromised sources**

- **Paid press releases** (PRNewswire, GlobeNewswire for crypto)
- **Anonymous Medium posts** without verification
- **Clickbait aggregators** (many .io domains)
- **YouTube thumbnails/titles** (verify content)
- **Sponsored content** disguised as news
- **Obvious shill accounts** on social media

---

## Source Evaluation Checklist

Before including a source, verify:

- [ ] Author is identified (not anonymous)
- [ ] Publication date is clear
- [ ] Claims are attributed to named sources
- [ ] No excessive promotional language
- [ ] Not tagged as "sponsored" or "partner content"
- [ ] Other outlets corroborate major claims

---

## Red Flag Indicators

Be skeptical if article:

- Uses excessive emojis or exclamation points
- Promises specific price targets
- Has affiliate links or referral codes
- Comes from newly created domain
- Has no author byline
- Is only found on one source (for major claims)

---

## Regional Source Considerations

| Region | Trusted Outlets |
|--------|-----------------|
| Asia | The Block (Asia), Forkast, WuBlockchain |
| Europe | The Block, CoinDesk, Reuters |
| Americas | Bloomberg, CoinDesk, The Block |

For region-specific regulatory news, prioritize local mainstream media.

---

## Updating This List

Crypto media landscape changes rapidly. Indicators a source should be downgraded:

- Acquired by project with conflict of interest
- Multiple retractions or corrections
- Departure of key editorial staff
- Shift to primarily sponsored content
